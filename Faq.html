Frequently asked questions

Can't find your answer here? Reach us on the contact page.

How fresh is the garri when it arrives?

Garri is sealed within 24 hours of processing and dispatched the same day your order is confirmed. Most domestic orders arrive within 2–4 business days; international orders vary by destination.

How does the "true measure" guarantee work?

Every scoop is calibrated against the same standard weight before packing. If a bag ever comes in under the listed weight, we'll replace it or refund that item, no questions asked.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — we regularly ship to the UK, US, and Canada. International shipping cost and timing are calculated at checkout based on destination and order weight.

How does the bulk discount work?

Add three or more staples to your cart and 10% is taken off your subtotal automatically — no code needed. Restaurants and diaspora stores can also ask about wholesale pricing via the contact page.

What if my order arrives damaged?

Send us a photo of the damaged item within 48 hours of delivery through the contact page and we'll arrange a replacement or refund right away.

Can I change or cancel an order after placing it?

Orders can be changed or cancelled within 2 hours of placing them, before they're packed for dispatch. Message us as soon as possible via the contact page.
