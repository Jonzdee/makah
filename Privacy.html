Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information we collect

When you place an order, subscribe to restock alerts, or contact us, we collect the details you provide directly — name, email, phone number, delivery address, and order history. We do not collect payment card numbers ourselves; these are handled by our payment processor.

2. How we use it

We use your information to process and deliver orders, respond to enquiries, send restock or order-status emails you've opted into, and improve our catalog and service. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Sharing with third parties

We share order details with delivery partners strictly to fulfill shipping, and with payment processors strictly to complete transactions. These partners are only given what they need to perform their service.

4. Cookies and site data

Our site uses only the technical data necessary to keep your cart working as you browse. We don't run third-party ad-tracking cookies.

5. Your choices

You can unsubscribe from restock emails at any time using the link in those emails, or request a copy or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at hello@makahserenityfoods.com.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@makahserenityfoods.com or via our contact page.
