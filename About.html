Our story

Started with one scoop, one true measure.

Makah Serenity Foods began in Ijebu Ode, packing garri for family abroad who missed the taste of home. What started as care packages grew into a standard: every scoop, every bag, weighed the same way, every time.

Our mission

Nigerian staples that taste like they came straight from the farm, no matter how far you've moved.

We work directly with growers and millers across Ogun, Oyo, and Benue states, cutting out the middle markets that erode both quality and fair pricing. Every batch is checked by hand before it's sealed — no shortcuts, no filler, no guesswork on weight.

How we got here

From care packages to a standard people trust

2018

Packing garri for family abroad

A weekend habit of weighing and sealing garri for relatives in London and Houston, so it arrived exactly as promised.

2020

First commercial batch, first scale

Word spread beyond family. We built our first calibrated scoop standard so every customer got the same fill, every time.

2023

Direct partnerships with growers

We cut out the middle markets and started buying straight from millers in Ogun, Oyo, and Benue — better pay for them, better price for you.

2026

Shipping to 12 countries, still by hand

Same-day dispatch, same hand-checked batches, now reaching Nigerian kitchens across three continents.

What guides us
